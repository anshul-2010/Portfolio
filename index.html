Dr. Sarah Chen

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science with a focus on machine learning and computer vision applications in medical imaging. My research explores novel deep learning architectures for improving diagnostic accuracy in radiology, with particular emphasis on domain adaptation techniques for cross-institutional data. I am seeking to advance my research through a Ph.D. program that bridges theoretical machine learning with practical healthcare applications, contributing to the development of trustworthy AI systems for clinical decision support.

Research Projects

My research focuses on advancing machine learning techniques for medical imaging, with emphasis on domain adaptation, explainable AI, and robust model generalization across diverse clinical settings.
