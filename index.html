Anshul Bagaria

Researcher | B.Tech. (Honors) Biological Engineering & M.Tech. Data Science

My research interest lies at the intersection of medical imaging, multimodal AI, and responsible AI, with a focus on building machine learning systems that are reliable, interpretable, and clinically meaningful.

I have worked on a wide range of problems, including domain adaptation for medical image segmentation, artifact and anomaly detection, cross-modal representation learning, and bias mitigation in large language models. My research experience spans IIT Madras, IISc, and several industry collaborations, giving me exposure to both fundamental ML research and real-world healthcare applications.

Recently, my work has expanded toward causal inference, clinical summarization, and multimodal reasoning, with an emphasis on improving trustworthiness in clinical decision support systems.
