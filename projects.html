Key Technical Projects

Selected course projects and technical implementations demonstrating expertise across machine learning, computational biology, and AI systems.

AMR-MoEGA: Hybrid Framework for Antimicrobial Resistance Evolution

Jan 2024 - May 2024

Developed a framework combining Genetic Algorithms with a Mixture of Experts (MoE) classifier for AMR genotype prediction. Processed genomic data via BWA, SAMtools, VCFtools to extract SNP features for ML-driven fitness evaluation. Simulated adaptive evolution with HGT-based crossover, achieving convergence toward high-resistance genotypes.

Genetic Algorithms Mixture of Experts Bioinformatics XAI

Results: Achieved robust genotype prediction with MoE classifier (Accuracy: 93.4%, MCC: 0.87, AUC-ROC: 0.95). Applied XAI (SHAP) and LLM embeddings (ESM) to interpret resistance drivers and quantify sequence-level drift.

Enhancing Privacy-Utility Trade-offs in DP-Adam via Correlated Noise

Jul 2024 - Nov 2024

Implemented BLT-correlated noise in DP-Adam, reducing gradient variance by 12%, while maintaining privacy. Analyzed the effect of direct vs. separate moment-wise noise injection on the estimators for faster convergence. Extended correlated noise mechanisms from SGD to adaptive optimizers, establishing a foundational framework.

Differential Privacy DP-Adam Privacy-Preserving ML Optimization

Results: Demonstrated improved model utility under stringent budgets (ϵ ≤ 2) with up to 8% accuracy gain over baseline.

CLIP Gemini LLM Flask Multimodal AI
